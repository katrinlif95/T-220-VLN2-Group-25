How to Sell Artwork

Selling artwork through ArtVault is designed to be simple, professional and accessible for both independent artists and galleries. Create a listing by providing artwork details, images and pricing information, then publish your artwork for collectors to discover and bid on.

ArtVault allows sellers to manage listings, monitor bidding activity and connect with potential buyers through a secure and streamlined platform. Each artwork page is carefully structured to highlight important details and create a refined presentation experience for collectors browsing the marketplace.

Our goal is to provide artists and galleries with a trusted digital space where creativity, visibility and quality presentation come together.
