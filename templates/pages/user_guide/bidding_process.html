Bidding Process

The bidding process on ArtVault is designed to be clear, secure and easy to follow. When you find an artwork you are interested in, you can place a bid directly from the artwork page and monitor its status from your account.

Once a bid has been submitted, it may remain pending until the seller reviews it. If your bid is accepted, you will be guided through the finalize bid process, where you can enter contact information, payment details and review your order before confirming the purchase.

If a bid is rejected or cancelled, the artwork may remain available for other collectors. ArtVault keeps the bidding flow organized so buyers can clearly see the status of their offers throughout the process.
